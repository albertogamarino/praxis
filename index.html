01 The Structural Shift Where the pressure is already visible
Diagnosis

Most organizations are optimizing a model being repriced beneath them.

AI compresses execution before organizations redesign roles, controls, evidence and accountability around it.

01

Execution accelerates. AI compresses drafting, analysis, research and synthesis. The workflow still assumes human-only production.

02

03

07 What This Is Not Positioning by exclusion
Exclusions

PRAXIS is workflow redesign for high-trust work. Not generic AI adoption.

Not AI tool implementation
PRAXIS does not start with a tool. It starts with the workflow — because the right AI role cannot be determined before the work is mapped. Tool selection follows from the Blueprint, not the other way around.
Not agent development by default
Agents are an output of the Blueprint, not an entry point. PRAXIS does not build agents into workflows that have not been redesigned around them. Where the Blueprint shows a dedicated AI capability is required, agents become a possible implementation layer.
Not adoption without redesign
Deploying AI into an existing workflow makes a poorly designed process faster. PRAXIS maps the workflow first, then defines where AI belongs inside a redesigned version of it. The sequence is not optional.
Start with the workflow

Do not scale AI into a workflow you have not mapped.

PRAXIS begins with one workflow, its artefacts, decision points, current AI exposure and the role AI should or should not play inside it.
